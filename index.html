The Bachelor's Chase

The Bachelor's Chase is an innovative, competent and reliable card game based on blackjack.

Control:

Keyboard: Name/Lobby/Chat type in
Enter: With login OK, with change of name Enter, with lobby choose Create / Join a new lobby, with send chat press button.

Counting system:

Points: Points scored by cards. These are reset to zero after every match.
Coins: Will be charged after every match. You can use this to throw cards away.
